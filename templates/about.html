Our Story

Echo Noir began with a simple observation: small business owners were constantly interrupted by phone calls, forcing them to choose between missing potential customers or breaking their workflow.

We realized that advancements in AI technology could solve this problem in a way that was both affordable and effective. After months of development and testing with small businesses across industries, Echo Noir was born.

Today, Echo Noir helps thousands of small businesses across the country provide exceptional customer service while allowing owners and employees to focus on what they do best.
